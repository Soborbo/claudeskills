# Headline Templates

## Hero H1 Formulas

### Formula 1: Outcome + Location
```
[Service] in [Location] — [Proof Point]
```
**Examples:**
- "House Removals Bristol — 4.98★ from 270+ Reviews"
- "Kitchen Fitting Birmingham — Free Design Consultation"

### Formula 2: Problem Solved
```
[Service] Without [Pain Point]
```
**Examples:**
- "Move House Without the Stress"
- "Get a New Boiler Without the Hassle"

### Formula 3: Speed/Ease + Outcome
```
Get [Outcome] in [Timeframe]
```
**Examples:**
- "Get Your Free Quote in 47 Seconds"
- "Book Your Cleaner in 2 Minutes"

### Formula 4: Differentiator Lead
```
The Only [Service] That [Unique Benefit]
```
**Examples:**
- "The Only Removals Company That Guarantees Your Date"
- "The Only Roofer Who Shows Up on Time"

## Section Headlines

### Problem Section
Lead with empathy, acknowledge pain:
- "Moving is stressful. We get it."
- "Tired of unreliable quotes?"
- "Worried about hidden costs?"

### Solution Section
Pivot to relief:
- "That's exactly why we exist"
- "Here's how we make it painless"
- "We do things differently"

### Benefits Section
Outcome-focused:
- "What You Get"
- "Why [X] Customers Choose Us"
- "The [Brand] Difference"

### How It Works
Process clarity:
- "3 Simple Steps to Your [Outcome]"
- "How It Works"
- "Getting Started is Easy"

### Social Proof
Trust-building:
- "Join [X]+ Happy Customers"
- "Don't Just Take Our Word For It"
- "What Our Customers Say"

### FAQ
Objection handling:
- "Common Questions"
- "Got Questions? We've Got Answers"
- "Everything You Need to Know"

### Final CTA
Urgency + reassurance:
- "Ready to [Outcome]?"
- "Get Started Today"
- "Let's Make This Happen"

## Subheadline Patterns

Subheadlines should expand on H1 with specifics:

**H1:** "House Removals Bristol"
**Sub:** "Fully insured, 5-star rated, free quotes in 60 seconds"

**H1:** "Get Your Free Quote in 47 Seconds"
**Sub:** "No hidden fees. No obligations. Just honest pricing."

## Headlines to Avoid

- ❌ "Welcome to [Company Name]"
- ❌ "About Our Services"
- ❌ Generic questions: "Looking for a plumber?"
- ❌ "We Are The Best At..."
- ❌ Anything starting with "Our" or "We"

## Localisation (UK)

- Use "colour" not "color"
- Use "neighbours" not "neighbors"
- Use "organised" not "organized"
- Reference UK-specific concerns (council, estate agents, etc.)
