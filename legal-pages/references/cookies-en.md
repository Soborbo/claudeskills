# Cookie Policy Template (UK English)

Replace all `[PLACEHOLDERS]` with actual business details.

```markdown
# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: [DATE]**

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary

Essential for the website to function. No consent needed.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `cf_clearance` | Cloudflare security | Session |
| `cookieyes-consent` | Your cookie preferences | 1 year |

### Analytics (with consent)

Help us understand how visitors use our site.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics visitor ID | 2 years |
| `_ga_*` | Google Analytics session | 2 years |

### Marketing (with consent)

Track advertising performance.

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `_gcl_au` | Google Ads conversion | 90 days |
| `_fbp` | Facebook Pixel | 90 days |

## Managing Cookies

### On our site

Click "Cookie Settings" in the footer to change your preferences.

### In your browser

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge)

## Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services:

| Service | Type | Privacy Policy |
|---------|------|----------------|
| Google Analytics | Analytics | policies.google.com/privacy |
| Google Ads | Marketing | policies.google.com/privacy |
| Facebook | Marketing | facebook.com/privacy |
| Cloudflare | Infrastructure | cloudflare.com/privacypolicy |

We don't control these cookies. See their privacy policies.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Check "Last updated" date.

## Contact

Questions about cookies? Email [EMAIL].
```

## Cookie Categories

| Analytics | Yes | Yes |

## Cookie Triggers

If ANY of these cookies are used, Cookie Policy is REQUIRED:

| Cookie | Category |
|--------|----------|
| `_ga`, `_ga_*` | Analytics |
| `_gid` | Analytics |
| `_fbp` | Marketing |
| `_gcl_au` | Marketing |
