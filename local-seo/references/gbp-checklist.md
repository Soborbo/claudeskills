## Basic Information

| Field | Requirement | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Business name | Exact legal name | "Bristol Removals Ltd" |
| Address | Full, verified | "123 High Street, Bristol, BS1 2AB" |
| Phone | Local +44 | "+44 117 123 4567" |
| Website | Homepage | "https://bristolremovals.co.uk" |
| Hours | Accurate | Mon-Fri 8am-6pm |

## Categories

| Type | Selection |
|------|-----------|
| Primary | Most specific service | "Moving Company" |
| Secondary | Related services (max 9) | "Office Movers", "Packing Service" |

**Primary category = most important ranking factor.**

## Business Description

- 750 characters maximum
- Primary keyword in first sentence
- Include location naturally
- List main services
- Call to action at end

```
Bristol Removals Ltd provides professional house and office 
removals across Bristol and surrounding areas. Our fully 
insured team handles local and long-distance moves with care. 
Services include packing, storage, and same-day availability. 
Contact us for a free quote.
```
