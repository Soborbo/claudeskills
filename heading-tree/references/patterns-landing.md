# Landing Page Heading Patterns

## H1 (Hero)

**Pattern:** `{Primary Keyword} - {Differentiator/Benefit}`

```
{Service} {Location} - {Value Prop}
```

| Example | Notes |
|---------|-------|
| "House Removals Bristol - Stress-Free Moving From £299" | Price anchor |
| "Office Cleaning London - Same-Day Service Available" | Urgency |
| "Emergency Plumber Manchester - 24/7 Call-Out" | Availability |

**Rules:**
- Primary keyword at start
- Location if local business
- Max 60 characters
- One H1 per page

## H2 Patterns by Section

### Problem

**Pattern:** `[Empathy Question]` or `[Pain Point Statement]`

| Example |
|---------|
| "Tired of Stressful House Moves?" |
| "Moving Doesn't Have to Be Overwhelming" |
| "The Hidden Costs of DIY Removals" |

### Solution

**Pattern:** `[How We] + [Solve Problem]`

| Example |
|---------|
| "How We Make Moving Simple" |
| "Your Complete Removals Solution" |
| "Professional Service, Personal Touch" |

### Benefits

**H2 Pattern:** `Why [Choose/Trust] [Us/Service]`

```
H2: "Why Bristol Families Choose Us"

H3s (one per benefit):
- "Fully Insured Removals"
- "Fixed Price Guarantee"
- "Same-Day Availability"
```

### How It Works

**H2 Pattern:** `How [Service] Works` or `[Number] Simple Steps`

```
H2: "3 Simple Steps to Your New Home"

H3s (one per step):
- "1. Get Your Free Quote"
- "2. We Pack & Load"
- "3. Relax in Your New Home"
```

### Social Proof

**Pattern:** `What [Customers] Say` or `[Rating] Rated [Service]`

| Example |
|---------|
| "What Our Customers Say" |
| "4.9★ Rated Removal Company" |
| "Trusted by 500+ Bristol Families" |

### Pricing

**H2 Pattern:** `[Service] Prices` or `Transparent Pricing`

```
H2: "House Removal Prices"

H3s (tiers):
- "Studio & 1 Bed Moves"
- "2-3 Bed House Moves"
- "Large Home Moves"
```

### FAQ

**H2 Pattern:** `[Service] FAQs` or `Common Questions`

```
H2: "House Removals FAQs"

H3s (questions - 6 typical):
- "How much does a house move cost?"
- "Do you provide packing materials?"
- "What areas do you cover?"
- "How far in advance should I book?"
- "Are you fully insured?"
- "Do you move pianos and heavy items?"
```

### Final CTA

**Pattern:** `[Action] + [Benefit]` or `[Ready Statement]`

| Example |
|---------|
| "Get Your Free Quote Today" |
| "Ready to Move?" |
| "Start Your Stress-Free Move" |
