# Service & Service-Area Page Patterns

## Service Page

### H1

**Pattern:** `{Service} {Location} - {Differentiator}`

| Example |
|---------|
| "Office Removals Bristol - Commercial Moving Experts" |
| "Packing Service Bristol - Professional & Careful" |
| "Furniture Storage Bristol - Secure & Flexible" |

### H2 Sections

```
H1: Office Removals Bristol - Commercial Moving Experts
  H2: Why Choose Our Office Removal Service
    H3: Minimal Business Disruption
    H3: IT Equipment Specialists
    H3: Weekend & Out-of-Hours Available
  H2: How Office Moves Work
    H3: 1. Free Site Survey
    H3: 2. Detailed Moving Plan
    H3: 3. Efficient Relocation
  H2: Office Removal Prices
    H3: Small Office (1-10 desks)
    H3: Medium Office (10-50 desks)
    H3: Large Office (50+ desks)
  H2: Office Removals FAQs
    H3: How long does an office move take?
    H3: Do you handle IT equipment?
  H2: Get Your Office Move Quote
```

## Service-Area Page

### H1

**Pattern:** `{Service} in {Location}`

| Example |
|---------|
| "House Removals in Bath" |
| "Man and Van in Weston-super-Mare" |
| "Office Removals in Gloucester" |

**Note:** Location is the AREA, not main business location.

### H2 Sections (Location-Heavy)

Include location in 2-3 H2s:

```
H1: House Removals in Bath
  H2: Why Choose Our Bath Removal Service
  H2: Moving Services in Bath
    H3: House Removals
    H3: Flat Moves
    H3: Packing Service
  H2: Areas We Cover Near Bath
  H2: Bath Removals FAQs
    H3: How much do Bath removals cost?
    H3: Do you cover all BA postcodes?
  H2: Get Your Bath Removal Quote
```

### Location Mention Rules

| Level | Location Mentions |
|-------|-------------------|
| H1 | Required (area name) |
| H2 | 2-3 mentions |
| H3 | Optional, natural only |

### Postcode/Neighborhood Mentions

For service-area pages, can mention specific areas:

```
H2: Areas We Cover Near Bath
  H3: Bathwick & Widcombe
  H3: Odd Down & Twerton
  H3: Surrounding Villages
```

## Service vs Service-Area Comparison

| Element | Service Page | Service-Area Page |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| H1 focus | Service type | Location |
| Location in H1 | Business location | Target area |
| H2 location mentions | 1-2 | 2-3 |
| Coverage section | Optional | Required |
| Local FAQs | Generic | Area-specific |
