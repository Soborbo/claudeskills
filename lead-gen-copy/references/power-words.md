## Words to Avoid

| Word | Problem | Use Instead |
|------|---------|-------------|
| Solutions | Meaningless | [Specific service] |
| Leverage | Corporate jargon | Use |
| Utilize | Pretentious | Use |
| Synergy | Buzzword | [Specific benefit] |
| Best-in-class | Unverifiable | [Specific proof] |
| Leading | Vague claim | [Market position] |
| Quality | Empty without proof | [Specific standard] |
| Cheap | Implies low value | Affordable |
| Cutting-edge | Cliché | [Specific technology] |

## Number Specificity

**Vague → Specific:**
- "Fast" → "Same-day"
- "Many customers" → "270+ customers"
- "High rated" → "4.98★ average"
- "Quick quotes" → "47-second quotes"
- "Experienced" → "22 years"

Specific numbers create trust. Round numbers feel made up.
